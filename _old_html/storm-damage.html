What We Fix After Toronto Storms

Active Leaks

Water entering your living space is a critical emergency. We dispatch immediately to tarp the area and stop further interior water damage before providing a permanent repair solution.

Wind Damage

High Toronto winds easily tear off aging shingles, leaving the underlayment exposed to rain.

Ice Dams

Winter ice buildup pushes water under shingles, requiring careful removal and sealing.

Tree Impact

Fallen branches can cause structural deck damage requiring emergency reinforcement before repair.

Hail Impact

Hail bruises shingles, leading to granule loss and premature failure over time.
