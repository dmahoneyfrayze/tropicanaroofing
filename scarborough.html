We're Your Scarborough Neighbors

When you hire Tropicana Roofing, you're not getting a faceless out-of-town crew. Our local office is right here at 215 Markham Road, and we’ve spent two decades working on homes in every corner of Scarborough.

We understand the unique roofing challenges of the area—from the wind loads coming off Lake Ontario in the bluffs, to the specific architectural styles common in Agincourt and Malvern. This local knowledge allows us to recommend roofing systems that actually last in our specific micro-climate.

Our reputation in Scarborough is everything to us. We rely on word-of-mouth from satisfied neighbors, which means we treat every roof replacement or repair like we're working on our own home. Quality craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and total cleanup are guaranteed.
