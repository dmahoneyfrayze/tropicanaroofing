Built on Toronto Rooftops, One Honest Job at a Time

What started as a small, two-man crew operating out of a garage in Scarborough has grown into one of the Greater Toronto Area's most respected roofing companies. But our core philosophy hasn't changed since day one.

We noticed early on that the roofing industry was plagued by high-pressure sales tactics and corner-cutting. We decided to be the exact opposite. We built Tropicana Roofing on clear communication, meticulous craftsmanship, and showing up when we promise.

Today, we're proud to say that a significant portion of our new business comes from referrals—neighbors telling neighbors about the roofer they actually trust.
